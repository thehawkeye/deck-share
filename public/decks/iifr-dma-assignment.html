Task 1

Diagnosis

You have the following data from the last 30 days:

  • 800 page sessions — 15% Google Search, 40% Meta, 30% LinkedIn, 15% direct
  • Form fill rate: 15% overall — but LinkedIn visitors convert at 22%, Google Search at 8%
  • Application start rate: 29% of form fillers begin an application
  • Application submit rate: 23% of those who start — drop-off is highest at the document upload step

You can only work on three things. Which three and why?
