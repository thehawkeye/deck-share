The Problem

Indian B-schools have a faculty crisis. Qualified practitioners have no pathway in.

NEP 2020 mandates industry linkage. B-schools are required to demonstrate practitioner faculty credentials. Yet the formal pathway into teaching for senior executives and domain experts does not exist.

Those who want to teach have no credible route. Those who need them cannot find them.

Methodology

The Demo Day is the product, not the event.

Every Demo Day is structured around a live twenty-five minute module segment. The attendee experiences what it feels like to be taught by a practitioner-educator. That experience converts.

Webinars Learning Tables Podcasts Thought Leadership Peer Referral Demo Days Email Nurture LinkedIn
  • 0-10 min The Problem We Solve Short talk with practitioner data. Why the practitioner faculty gap exists and what it costs B-schools.
  • 10-35 min The Live Module Demo A real twenty-five minute segment from the BECP curriculum. The actual experience of being a participant.
  • 35-45 min The Credential EFMD Candidate Institution. What it means and what it enables for your academic practice.
  • 45-75 min Peer Story and Open Q&A BECP graduate conversation. Open questions. 1:1 interest form. Module demo recording captured as content asset.
