The Three Changes

What the Council of Experts changed.

  • Change One Two-track audience strategy BECP and BEFM are different products with different buyers and conversion triggers. BECP's buyer: mid-career educators and L&D professionals. BEFM's buyer: C-Suite. These are different people.
  • Change Two Demo Day is the product It must be a live 25-minute pedagogical experience — not a panel, not a keynote, not slides. Every attendee walks out having experienced what it feels like to be taught by a practitioner-educator.
  • Change Three BEFM enters the pipeline in July The BECP graduate is the most credible BEFM prospect. BEFM conversion starts after BECP cohort is enrolled — seeded by peer invitation.

Core Plan Headlines
  • ■
    Build the category before selling the programme. Category: "Practitioner Faculty Pathway" — IIFR owns this space before anyone else frames it.

    Two products, two buyers, two timelines — one mission. BECP (Rs 4-6L, 8-day pilot July 2026) targets mid-career educators and L&D professionals. BEFM (Rs 25-30L, 3-year launch September 2026) targets C-Suite and senior executives.

  • ■
    Email capture is non-negotiable at every touchpoint. Every paid ad, webinar, content download, and Demo Day registration captures email before confirmation. No email, no follow-up path.

Strategic Logic

Three forces have converged.

  • Force One NEP 2020 Implementation B-schools are mandated to demonstrate industry linkage. Practitioner faculty with formal credentials fulfill this requirement in a way informal adjuncts do not.
  • Force Two Post-Peak Executive Plateaus The cohort of CXOs who scaled Indian consumer internet (2012-2022) is now 45-55, looking for next chapters. Legacy is a real trigger.
  • Force Three EFMD's Asian Expansion EFMD's geographic broadening creates a window for an India-origin institution to pursue certification before the category is crowded with EFMD-certified competitors.

Messaging Architecture

Three threads. One mission.

  • Thread One Boardroom to Lecture Hall Your experience is your qualification. The boardroom taught you things no PhD programme can. Now teach the next generation of leaders.
  • Thread Two Pracademic Credentials Not a hobby. A recognised academic pathway. EFMD certification means global credibility for your faculty practice.
  • Thread Three Learning to Teach Teaching is a craft. Learn it deliberately. Your first class — and your hundredth — will be better for it.

Three Scenarios
Optimistic
BECP pilot oversubscribed by mid-career educators. EFMD confirms by Jun 2026. 2-3 senior CXOs express public BEFM intent.
Accelerate BEFM pipeline. Consider second BECP cohort. Announce EFMD formally.
Base Case
BECP fills 15-20 from mid-career + HR segment. EFMD confirmation slips to late 2026. B-school outreach yields 3-5 institutional commitments.
Execute peer referral from base cohort. Extend BECP waitlist into 2027 cohort. Maintain EFMD aspiration framing.
Downside
BECP pilot underenrolled (under 10). EFMD certification denied or materially delayed. Senior executive demand does not materialise.
Pivot BECP messaging to explicit "practitioner credential for educators" (drop CXO identity frame). Assess whether BEFM launches Sep 2026 or defers to 2027.
